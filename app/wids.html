Women in Data Science (WiDS), Delhi

24th Feb, 2018, Dogra Hall

The Women in Data Science (WiDS) conference is held annually globally at 100+ locations. WiDS is the result of partnerships between the Stanford Institute for Computational and Mathematical Engineering (ICME) and other technology focused organizations.
The WiDS Conference aims to inspire and educate data scientists worldwide, regardless of the gender, and support women in the field. This annual one-day technical conference provides an opportunity to hear about the latest data science related research and applications in a broad set of domains. WiDS will be held in India at Indian Institute of Technology Delhi on 24 th Feb 2018. Tryst is IIT Delhi’s technical festivals. Tryst 2018 revolves around the theme ‘Infinity and Beyond’ as we strive to celebrate the limitless capabilities of this world in Data Sciences.
For the first time, Tryst has partnered with Stanford University for the WiDS conference. This event is expected to pull audience from 300+ prestigious technical colleges and industry.
