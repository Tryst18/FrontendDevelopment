TRYST'18 ACCOMMODATION POLICY

Accommodation facilities will be provided from 23rd Feb’18 (12pm) to 27th Feb’18 (11am)​ only.

PROCEDURE FOR REGISTRATION

  • Register yourself at www.tryst-iitd.com by 22nd February​ 2018​. Online requests after this date will not be considered, though limited offline registration facility will be available on the onset of the festival. Registrations will be entertained on first come first serve basis.

  • Accommodation confirmation receipt will only be received after the complete payment has been made.

  • Report at the Accommodation Desk during arrival with the confirmation mail printout and a college ID. Further directions will then be provided.

ACCOMMODATION CHARGES

  • The participants will be accommodated in well-constructed hostel common rooms.

  • The accommodation provided will be on sharing basis.

  • Accommodation facilities for boys and girls will be separate.

  • Facility of accommodation for faculty is not provided separately.

  • Charges for Accommodation per person are applicable as:
    • Rs.400 for 1 day or less
    • Rs.700 for 2 days
    • Rs.1000 for 3 days
    • Rs.1200 for 4 days

  • Accommodation Desk timings: 9:00am to 6:00pm

  • An extra amount of Rs 100 per person will be charged for offline registrations.

  • If you are arriving or leaving at any time other than these, kindly inform the accommodation team beforehand via email.

TERMS AND CONDITIONS

  • Check Out time: before 11:00 am and Check in Time: after 12:00 noon .

  • The charges include only breakfast in the respective hostels allotted. Lunch/Dinner will not be provided.

  • The participants have to pay in advance. Once registered no refund will be made under any circumstances.

  • For extending your stay, accommodation will be provided subject to availability.

IMPORTANT GUIDELINES

  • Please ensure that you have received a confirmation mail after the payment.

  • By no means will more people than approved earlier will be accommodated in the campus.

  • Tryst would not be responsible if the services do not meet certain expectations. (Each individual may have different paying capacity and expectations, therefore we would try to set a minimum benchmark to provide these services).

  • The participants are advised to stay strictly for the same number of days for which they have paid. Else, a fine of Rs 1000 will be charged per extra day of stay.

  • If the team/person fails to arrive 12 hrs from the registered time of arrival without prior notice, the booking will be held cancelled without any refund.

  • Alcohol, smoking, drugs and explosives of any kind are strictly prohibited inside the campus.

  • Illegal activities are strictly prohibited on the campus. You are required to follow the rules and regulations of the hostel and locality. Random checks would be made to avoid any illegal activities at the campus. Any team/participant not abiding by these rules would be liable to security action and a fine of Rs 1000 would be charged.
    NOTE​ : ​In case of any disputes, the decision of Tryst Accommodation Team will be final.

GENERAL INFORMATION

  • Participants are advised to carry a small lock and chain for safety of their luggage as no lockers or any other storage facility is provided.

  • Loss of any luggage is not the responsibility of Accommodation Team.

  • Accommodation IDs, valid student id-card and gate passes should be carried at all times. You may be asked to produce it by any member of Tryst organizing team.

  • Those carrying their laptops or similar electronic gadgets will have to declare their belongings which could be checked for verification.

  • We shall provide you with mattresses and other essentials. However, you are encouraged to carry your own blankets.

  • At the time of check-out, the participants are required to return all the commodities provided to them

  • For any other queries, drop a mail at accommodation@tryst-iitd.com or contact +91 8862577042
